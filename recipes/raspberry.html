Raspberry-White Chocolate Chip Muffins

Description

Here is a recipe of my favorite snack: raspberry-white chocolate chip muffins!

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cake flour
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/2 cup miniature white chocolate chips
  • 3 tbsp seedless raspberry jam

Topping:

  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup miniature white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). grease and flour a 12-cup muffin tin or use paper liners.
  2. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder and baking soda together in a large bowl.
  3. Combine buttermilk, egg vanilla extract together in a small bowl and whisk.
  4. Ad buttermilk mixture and melted butter to flour mixture, whisking until just moistened; batter will be lumpy. Fold in raspberries, miniature white chocolate chips, and raspberry jam.
  5. Fill muffin cups with batter.
  6. Combine sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Sprinkle topping on muffind. Top with white chocolate chips.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean, 18-20 minutes.
  8. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a rack to cool completely, about 30 minutes more.
