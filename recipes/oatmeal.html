Soft Oatmeal Cookies

Description

Make soft oatmeal cookies in 2 hours!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3 cups quick cooking oats

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, cream together butter, white sugar and brown sugar. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Combine flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon; stir into the creamed mixture. Mix in oats. Cover, and chill dough for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets. Roll dough into walnut sized balls, and place 2 inches apart on cookie sheets. Flatten each cookie with a large fork dipped in sugar.
  3. Bake for 8-10 minutes in preheated oven.
  4. Allow cookies to cool on baking sjeet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
